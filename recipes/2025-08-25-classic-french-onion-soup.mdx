# Classic French Onion Soup

This classic French onion soup features slowly caramelized onions that cook for 45 minutes to develop deep, meaty flavors. The result is a rich, comforting soup with an intense depth of flavor that's perfect for cold days. Traditionally served with cheesy garlic bread or gruyere cheese toasts.

**Servings:** 6  
**Category:** Entrees

## Notes

Onions cook low and slow for about 45 minutes to draw out their natural sweetness, creating deep, meaty flavors. Can substitute wine with extra beef stock if preferred. This recipe creates restaurant-quality French onion soup at home.
