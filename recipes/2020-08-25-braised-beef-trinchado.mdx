# Braised Beef (Trinchado)

This traditional Portuguese dish features tender cubed beef braised in a rich sauce of Worcestershire, wine, and spices. The beef is marinated to enhance the flavors, then slowly simmered until fork-tender. Perfect served with crusty Portuguese bread rolls.

**Servings:** 3
